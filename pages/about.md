## About the Collection

This is a collection of postcards from the early 1900s. These postcards are an interesting glimpse into how friends contacted one another in the early 1900s, as well as showcasing some popular humor from that time. The postcards are believed to be copyright-free, being that the postcards were made before 1929, are believed to be public-domain and copyright-free under the laws of the United States, but a determination was not made as to their copyright status under the copyright laws of other countries. These pictures are available to download as JPGs.
